Description

The pasta is an italian food

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Four to six quarts of cold water
  • 1-2 tablespoons of salt

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a full, rolling boil. Before adding the pasta,salt to the boiling water. If you don't season the cooking water, the pasta may taste flat—no matter how salty the sauce may be.   2. Stir gently. Short pasta should be stirred immediately after adding it to water, but let spaghetti and long strands soften for a minute before stirring. Leave the pot uncovered while the pasta cooks.
